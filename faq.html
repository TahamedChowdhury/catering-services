Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our catering services

How far in advance should I book your catering services?

+

We recommend booking our services as soon as you have your event date secured. For peak seasons (May-September and December), we suggest booking 6-12 months in advance. For smaller events during non-peak times, 2-3 months notice is usually sufficient. However, we occasionally have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to contact us even for short-notice events.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

+

To provide you with an accurate quote, we need the following details:

  • Event date and time
  • Event location
  • Approximate guest count
  • Type of event
  • Service style preference (buffet, plated, family-style, etc.)
  • Any specific menu preferences or dietary requirements
  • Any additional services needed (bar service, staffing, rentals, etc.)

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and allergies?

+

Absolutely! We're experienced in accommodating various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and most food allergies. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions during the planning process, and we'll create appropriate menu options. For severe allergies, we take extra precautions in our food preparation but do recommend discussing specific concerns directly with our chef.

Do you offer menu tastings?

+

Yes, we offer menu tastings for confirmed clients who have signed a contract and paid a deposit. Tastings typically include samples of your selected appetizers, entrées, and sides. For wedding catering packages over a certain value, we offer complimentary tastings for up to 4 people. For smaller events, tastings are available for a fee that can be applied to your final balance if you proceed with booking.

Can we customize our menu?

+

Absolutely! Custom menus are our specialty. While we have sample menus to inspire you, we're happy to create a bespoke menu tailored to your preferences, theme, or cultural background. Our executive chef enjoys the creative challenge of designing unique menus that reflect your personal taste and event vision.

Do you provide serving staff for events?

+

Yes, we provide professional service staff including servers, bartenders, and event captains for your event. Our staff is professionally trained, uniformed, and experienced in providing exceptional service. The number of staff required depends on your guest count, menu selections, and service style. We typically recommend one server per 20-25 guests for plated service and one server per 30-35 guests for buffet service.

What is included in your basic catering package?

+

Our standard catering package includes:

  • Food preparation and delivery
  • Professional service staff for setup, food service, and cleanup
  • Standard white china plates, stainless steel flatware, and glassware
  • White linen napkins
  • Buffet setup with stainless chafing dishes and serving utensils
  • Water service for guests

Additional items such as specialty rentals, bar service, decorative elements, and cake cutting can be added to your package for an additional fee.

What is your typical price range?

+

Our pricing varies based on several factors including menu selection, service style, guest count, event date, and location. As a general guideline:

  • Buffet service typically ranges from $45-75 per person
  • Plated service typically ranges from $60-100 per person
  • Family-style service typically ranges from $55-85 per person
  • Cocktail receptions with heavy hors d'oeuvres typically range from $40-70 per person

These ranges include food and basic service but exclude bar service, specialty rentals, and service charges. We're happy to work with various budgets and can customize a package to meet your needs.

What is your payment schedule?

+

Our standard payment schedule is as follows:

  • 25% non-refundable deposit to secure your date and our services
  • 50% of the remaining balance due 60 days before your event
  • Final balance due 14 days before your event, along with final guest count

For events booked less than 60 days in advance, an adjusted payment schedule will be provided. We accept payments via credit card, bank transfer, or check.

Are there any additional fees I should be aware of?

+

In addition to the base catering cost, potential additional fees might include:

  • Service charge (20% of food and beverage total)
  • Travel fee for venues outside our standard service area
  • Extended service hours (events running longer than 5 hours)
  • Site visits for complex venue layouts
  • Special equipment rentals for venues without kitchen facilities
  • Additional staffing for complex service requirements

We're transparent about all costs and will clearly outline any additional fees in your proposal and contract.

Do you provide rentals such as tables, chairs, and linens?

+

Yes, we can coordinate all necessary rentals for your event including tables, chairs, specialty linens, tableware, tents, and decorative items. We work with trusted rental companies to ensure quality and reliability. While we handle the ordering and coordination, rental items are typically billed separately and may require their own contracts and deposits.

What happens to leftover food after the event?

+

Due to health department regulations and food safety concerns, we cannot leave perishable food items that have been out for service. However, any food that hasn't been put out for service can be packaged for you to take home. We're also proud to partner with local food recovery programs to donate eligible unused food whenever possible, in accordance with safe food handling guidelines.
